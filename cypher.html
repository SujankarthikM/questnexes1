Welcome to the Vigenère Cipher Challenge!

In this challenge, you'll be diving into the world of cryptography to crack a classic cipher. The Vigenère cipher is a technique that uses a keyword to encrypt text, adding a level of complexity that requires more than just a simple shift to decode. Your mission: decode the hidden message by discovering the right keyword and unraveling the cipher! Think you’re up for the challenge? Let’s see if you can crack the code and uncover the secret!

Example:

Keyword: LEMON

Word: HELLO

Encrypted: SIXZB

Keyword: BABLU
